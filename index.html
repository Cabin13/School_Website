Campus Life

Center of Performing Arts

Established on March 14, 2013.

The Center of Peforming Arts contains the Wanjoo Cafe and the Auditorium, along with several music rooms. It is mostly used for assemblies and co-curriculars.




Year 5/6 Building

Established on August 31, 2016.

The first floor in the Year 5/6 Building is for the Year 5's, the second floor is for Year 6's, and the third floor is for extra classes and is mostly used by the Senior School.




Chapel

Established on December 10, 1989.

The Chapel is used weekly for services, which are held seperately for Senior and Junior school.
